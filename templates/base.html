Shop Green Policies

Delivery Policy

At Shop Green, we are committed to delivering your orders efficiently and reliably. Our standard delivery times are as follows:

Processing Time:

Orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days. For custom-made items, such as fiberglass planters, 3 to 4 working days for processing and shipping. Customizations may require additional time.

Shipping Time:

Once processed, shipping times depend on the destination.

Shipping Costs:

Shipping costs are calculated based on the size, weight, and destination of your order. These costs will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Delivery Estimates:

Delivery times are estimates provided by the carrier and may vary due to factors such as weather conditions or carrier delays.

Order Issues:

If you encounter any issues with your delivery, such as delays or damage, please contact our customer service team promptly. We are here to assist you and ensure your order arrives as expected.

Thank you for shopping with Shop Green. We strive to provide a seamless delivery experience and appreciate your patience and understanding.

Mission

At Shop Green, our mission is to enrich your living spaces with high-quality, sustainable, and beautifully designed plants, planters, and home décor. We are dedicated to offering a curated selection of products that blend style, functionality, and environmental responsibility. Our goal is to inspire and support a greener lifestyle, making it easy for our customers to cultivate their own vibrant, eco-friendly environments. Through exceptional customer service and a commitment to innovation, we strive to be your trusted partner in creating and enjoying a more sustainable and aesthetically pleasing home.
